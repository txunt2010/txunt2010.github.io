Conclusion:¶

The data on hand only provided a glimpse of the survival rates among the different passengers on board the Titanic. The sample that was made available only had 891 instances out of the overall population of 2224. As a result, we can not conclude that there's a direct causation out of any perceived correlations. This dataset leaves me wanting to have access to a complete portion to further investigate the different variables that I was interested in. It leaves me wondering if females continued having a larger percentage of survival and whether or not the Upper Ticket Class Section continued having a better rate of survival.¶
